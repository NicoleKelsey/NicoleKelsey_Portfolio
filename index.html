Internal Ticket Desk System

Role
Lead UX Designer
Timeline
8 Weeks
Client
Internal Tool
Type
SaaS / Internal

The Problem

Employees faced significant friction when logging support requests across multiple departments, leading to delayed resolution times and lack of transparency in the ticket lifecycle.

UX Process & Insights

  • Employee Feedback: Identified manual entry and poor categorization as primary pain points.
  • Cross-Department Mapping: Designed a unified system that handles diverse requests from IT to HR within one interface.
Final Outcome

Streamlined the internal support workflow, providing employees with a clear, efficient way to manage and track requests from submission to resolution.
